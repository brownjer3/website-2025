To generate the OG image:

1. Visit http://localhost:3000/og-image-generator
2. Take a screenshot of the page (1200x630px)
3. Save as /public/og-image.jpg or /public/og-image.png
4. Delete the /src/app/og-image-generator folder after

Alternatively, use a tool like:
- https://og-image.vercel.app/
- https://bannerbear.com/
- Figma/Canva to create a 1200x630px image
