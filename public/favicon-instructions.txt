To generate favicons:

1. Create a 512x512px icon with your initials "JSB" or "JB" with a modern design
2. Use a tool like https://realfavicongenerator.net/ or https://favicon.io/

Required files to generate:
- favicon.ico (multi-size icon)
- favicon-16x16.png
- favicon-32x32.png
- apple-touch-icon.png (180x180)
- android-chrome-192x192.png
- android-chrome-512x512.png

Design suggestions:
- Use gradient background matching website theme (purple/blue)
- White or light text
- Clean, modern typography

Color scheme:
- Background: #8b5cf6 to #3b82f6 gradient
- Text: #ffffff
- Or use dark background with light accent
